Rome

Rome is located in the central portion of the Italian peninsula, on the Tiber River about 15 miles (24 km) inland from the Tyrrhenian Sea. Once the capital of an ancient republic and empire whose armies and polity defined the Western world in antiquity and left seemingly indelible imprints thereafter, the spiritual and physical seat of the Roman Catholic Church, and the site of major pinnacles of artistic and intellectual achievement, Rome is the Eternal City, remaining today a political capital, a religious centre, and a memorial to the greatest achievements of the past.

Nairobi

the bustling capital of Kenya, offers a unique blend of city life and wildlife experiences. It is the only city in the world with a national park, Nairobi National Park, where visitors can spot lions, rhinos, and giraffes against the backdrop of the city skyline. The city is also home to the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust, famous for its elephant orphanage, and the Giraffe Centre, where tourists can interact with endangered Rothschild giraffes. For a dose of culture and history, the Karen Blixen Museum and Bomas of Kenya showcase colonial heritage and traditional Kenyan life

New York

Iconic Landmarks

Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island: A symbol of freedom and democracy, visiting the Statue of Liberty and the nearby Ellis Island Immigration Museum is an unforgettable experience.

Empire State Building: This iconic skyscraper offers stunning views of the city from its observation decks.

World-Class Museums and Art

The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met): One of the largest and most prestigious art museums in the world, featuring over 5,000 years of art from every culture.

The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA): Home to famous works like Van Gogh's Starry Night and an incredible collection of modern and contemporary art.

Broadway Shows and Theater

Broadway: NYC is the world's theater capital, home to dozens of famous theaters offering musicals, dramas, and comedies. Must-see shows include classics like The Lion King, Hamilton, and Wicked.

Off-Broadway: For more experimental or intimate performances, Off-Broadway shows offer an alternative but equally impressive theater experience.
