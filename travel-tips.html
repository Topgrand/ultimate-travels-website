travel hacks and tips

For a smoother travel experience, here are some quick hacks and tips: Pack light: Use packing cubes and roll clothes to save space. Travel insurance: Always have it for peace of mind. Offline maps: Download maps and important info before traveling to avoid data charges. Local currency: Carry a small amount of local cash for tips and small purchases. Early flights: Choose morning flights to avoid delays. Reusable water bottle: Stay hydrated and reduce plastic waste. Wi-Fi spots: Save money by using free Wi-Fi at cafes or public spaces.
